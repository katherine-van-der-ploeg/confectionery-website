About Beau's

It all began one faithful night when Beau started hallucinating from lack of sleep due to her final exams in College. You see, Beau had been pulling all nighters for weeks in an attempt to prepare for her veterinary exams. As Beau watched zombie bunnies dance across her textbook for the eighth ninth time she realized that there had to be more to life than this. Thus, Beau dropped out of veterinary school and decided to set out on her own path of surgery sweetness. Were people pleased with her decision? No, but she did it anyway. After a year of soul searching Beau realized her love for confection and began studying candy making around the world eventually forming the business we all know and love!

  • Beautiful Hand made sweets

    Just because Beau couldn’t take the heat from vet school, doesn’t mean she has lost her love of helping others. Beau hosts a plethora of events aimed at raising money for local charities. Everything from a craft fair to a drag show, Beau’s has probably featured it!
